Real & Ready Rafting Company

The mission of Real & Ready Rafting Company is to be africa foremost and leading rafting company. Enabling rafting adventures in a secured and sustainable manner all the time. Our core values are: Collaborating and working with our clients to ensure they enjoy their rafting adventures. Showing adequate Respect for our clients, our rafting equipment and the environment. We observe the highest level of Integrity in dealing with all our clients. We follow and maintain all industry and working Safety policies and procedures in all our operations. Best Performance is always our watchword.

History

Real & Ready Rafting Company was established on 30th April 2012 as a subsidiary of Real & Ready groups with a staff strength of 5 but has since blossom to a company of more than 30 staff. At Real & Ready Rafting, we understand that the difference between success and failure often lies in the determination to persevere and stay focused, even in the face of challenges. As a company, we are committed to meeting our customers' needs for additional products at highly competitive prices, driven by our clear ambition to establish ourselves as a fast-growing and influential brand.
